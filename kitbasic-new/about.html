Montchanin is perfect for overnight stays, events, holiday parties, and parent visits to West Chester University — or even a staycation in West Chester, PA. Established in 1860, Montchanin is a Victorian inn set atop a hill overlooking the wooded 2-acre Brandywine Valley property.

Location

  • 25 miles south of Philadelphia
  • Driving distance from D.C.
  • Located in the heart of the Brandywine Valley

Features

  • Seven guest rooms, including two suites with fireplaces;
  • Details such as Monticello windows and mahogany millwork;
  • Large common areas, including a library and winter porch;
  • Outdoor space, including a wrap-around porch and brick patio
