Policies

Montchanin will make every attempt to be flexible in accommodating all guests' needs

CHECK-IN & CHECK-OUT

Check-in time is 4-8 p.m.
Check-out time is 11 a.m.

CANCELLATIONS

Reservations must be secured with a credit card or deposit. Cancellations are billed a 10% administrative fee. If the cancellation occurs less than 72 hours before the stay is to begin, one night's lodging per night booked, including tax, will be charged.

BREAKFAST SCHEDULE

Coffee is available at 7:30 a.m. and breakfast is served at 9 a.m., however, we are happy to accommodate early breakfast requests if you let us know before your stay.

DIETARY RESTRICTIONS

Faunbrook gladly accommodates dietary restrictions for breakfast if notified prior to your stay.

CHILDREN

Some parents may find Faunbrook unsuitable for small children. Please discuss details with us.

SMOKE-FREE

Due to the historic nature of the house, smoking is not permitted inside. Guests may smoke outside on the veranda.

PET-FREE

We regret we cannot accept pets but can recommend a "Pet Hotel" to guests.

DISCOUNTS

Receive discounted room prices for multi-night stays! Follow us on Facebook for flash sales and sign up for our e-mails for special offers.
