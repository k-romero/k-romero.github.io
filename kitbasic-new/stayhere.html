We do everything we can to make you feel at home when you stay at Montchanin. We invite you to take advantage of the whole experience during your overnight stay.

SETTLE IN

Check in during your pre-arranged arrival time and head up to your room. Let us know if we can provide you with anything to make your stay more comfortable.

UNWIND

Head down to the winter porch for a glass of complementary wine — if the weather's nice, explore the patio. If not, cozy up by the fire in the library.

DINE OUT

Indulge in dinner out at one of the many restaurants in downtown West Chester, just a mile away. Let us know if you'd like us to make reservations for you prior to your stay.

REST

Head back to Montchanin for a nice hot shower or soak in the tub, indulge in a good book, and relax. Enjoy a good night's sleep.

INDULGE

Mosey on down for breakfast at 9. We love using local ingredients for a farm-to-table breakfast experience. Early riser? Coffee's ready for you in the dining room at 7:30am. We're happy to accommodate early breakfast requests and dietary restrictions if notified prior to your stay.

EXPLORE

Head out to explore local attractions — restaurants, events, and shopping.
